JLAB Final Project - Wireless Power Transfer

Sahil Patel, Chris Brancato, Jialun Bao

About

We creatd a circuit that is able to wirelessly transmit power. Our circuit involves the following components:

  • Wien Bridge Oscillator
  • Power Amplifier
  • Pulse width modualtor
  • Transmitter and Receiver coils
  • Full wave bridge rectifier
  • Voltage regulator
  • H-bridge motor driver circuit

We set out to drive a DC motor with our circuit, however we were not able to transmit enough power for the DC motor to operate. Therefore, we demonstrate our wireless transmission of power via an LED hooked up to the H-bridge circuit.
